# I do Too Crew - n8n Build Order

## Objective

Build the automation stack in a sequence that creates value early and reduces complexity.

## Phase 1: Lead Engine

### 1. WhatsApp Lead Capture
Reason:
- Fastest business value
- Captures real prospects immediately
- Feeds Airtable with live data

### 2. Website Lead Capture
Reason:
- Keeps web and WhatsApp in one funnel
- Lets the future website plug in cleanly

### 3. Lead Follow-Up Automation
Reason:
- Prevents lead leakage early
- Makes inbound inquiries operationally useful

## Phase 2: Content Engine

### 4. Content Idea Generator
Reason:
- Builds planning momentum
- Gives the team content backlog quickly

### 5. Weekly Planner
Reason:
- Helps maintain consistent content generation

### 6. Content Production Generator
Reason:
- Turns ideas into assets and drafts

## Phase 3: Control Layer

### 7. Approval Router
Reason:
- Keeps human review in the system
- Prevents accidental publishing

### 8. Revision Workflow
Reason:
- Makes feedback actionable
- Reduces manual rewrite effort

## Phase 4: Distribution

### 9. Scheduled Publishing
Reason:
- Automates release after approvals are stable

## Phase 5: Visibility

### 10. Reporting Workflow
Reason:
- Adds performance visibility once activity is happening

## Recommended First Sprint

Build these first:
1. Airtable base
2. WhatsApp lead capture workflow
3. Website lead capture workflow
4. Lead follow-up automation

## Recommended Second Sprint

1. Content idea generator
2. Weekly planner
3. Content production generator

## Recommended Third Sprint

1. Approval router
2. Revision workflow
3. Scheduled publishing

## Recommended Fourth Sprint

1. Reporting workflow
2. performance refinements
3. analytics dashboards
