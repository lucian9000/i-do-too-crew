# I Do Crew - Advertising Brainstorm

## Positioning

I Do Crew should not position itself only as a matric dance planner. The stronger positioning is:

**Styled event planning for milestone moments**

Primary service lanes:
- Weddings
- Matric events
- Other milestone and private events
- Corporate and styled functions

This gives the brand a broader emotional range, stronger premium positioning, more content variety, and access to both aspirational youth audiences and higher-value wedding clients.

## WhatsApp Bot Role in the Funnel

Advertising should route traffic into WhatsApp and website forms.

The WhatsApp bot should ask:
1. What event are you planning?
2. What type of event is it?
3. Event date?
4. Location?
5. Guest count?
6. Budget range?
7. What help do you need?
8. Name and contact details

Then the workflow should:
- Save the lead to Airtable
- Tag the lead by event type and urgency
- Trigger quote follow-up or human handoff

## Website Structure Recommendation

- Planning a wedding?
- Planning a matric dance?

Recommended pages:

- Weddings
- Matric Dances
- Packages
- Gallery
- About
- Contact / Quote Form
- FAQ

## Strategic Recommendation

The advertising system should operate like this:
1. Inspiration content creates attention
2. Trust content removes doubt
3. WhatsApp bot and forms capture intent
4. Airtable stores and qualifies leads
5. n8n routes follow-up and content operations

This creates a repeatable acquisition and conversion machine rather than a simple posting workflow.
