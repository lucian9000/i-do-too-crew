# I Do Crew - Content Pillars

## Brand Position

**Styled event planning for milestone moments**

I Do Crew operates across two emotional and commercial lanes:
- Weddings
- Matric dances

## Pillar 1: Inspiration
Purpose:
- Reach
- Saves
- Shares
- Vision-building

Content examples:
- Theme ideas
- Colour palettes
- Venue styling concepts
- Moodboards
- Dream event setups

## Pillar 2: Transformation
Purpose:
- Show execution ability
- Demonstrate value visually

Content examples:
- Before and after reveals
- Decor changes
- Lighting upgrades
- Venue makeovers

## Pillar 3: Education
Purpose:
- Build authority
- Help planners feel safer and more informed

Content examples:
- Checklists
- Budget tips
- Booking order advice
- Common mistakes
- Planning timelines

## Pillar 4: Trust
Purpose:
- Reduce hesitation
- Show professionalism

Content examples:
- Testimonials
- Process walkthroughs
- Behind the scenes
- Team intros
- FAQs
- Package breakdowns

## Pillar 5: Emotion
Purpose:
- Increase emotional resonance
- Make the brand memorable

Content examples:
- Love stories
- Milestone moments
- Entrance reveals
- Memory-driven captions
- Family and celebration moments

## Pillar 6: Conversion
Purpose:
- Create inquiries
- Drive WhatsApp and quote form submissions

Content examples:
- Limited offers
- Package spotlights
- Quote CTAs
- Availability updates
- Call-to-book posts
