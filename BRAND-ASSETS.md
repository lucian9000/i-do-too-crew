## Watermark Rules

If used as a watermark on generated images or short videos:
- keep opacity low enough to avoid clutter
- place consistently in a corner or subtle center overlay
- use a transparent export where possible
- do not obstruct faces, decor focal points, or product details
- create one watermark version for light backgrounds and one for dark backgrounds
